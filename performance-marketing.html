Performance Marketing

Performance marketing works best when media buying, message, landing-page experience, and reporting all support the same commercial goal. It is not only about switching campaigns on inside an ad account. It is about making sure the full path from first click to enquiry or sale feels relevant, efficient, and commercially useful.

For ROUNDROCK COTTON, this means planning channel strategy, managing paid social and search activity, improving page alignment, and keeping reporting close to real business decisions. We work with widely used platforms such as Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, YouTube placements, Google Analytics 4, Google Tag Manager, Search Console, and other established tools where they are relevant to the project scope.

What this service solves

Performance usually breaks down when channel setup, ad message, landing-page experience, tracking, and reporting are handled as separate tasks by different people. The result is wasted spend, weak lead quality, and unclear reporting. We solve for the full acquisition path, not just ad account activity, so the marketing budget is tied more closely to real enquiries, qualified leads, and commercial growth.

Real business value

The business value is clearer budget control, lower acquisition waste, better lead quality, faster learning cycles, and reporting that helps managers and owners understand what is actually driving results across Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn activity, and supporting landing pages.

What is included

A connected acquisition system rather than isolated channel management.

Paid social campaigns

We plan and manage paid social campaigns across Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, and other relevant platforms with a focus on audience quality, creative testing, lead intent, and stronger alignment between the ad promise and the offer being promoted.

Search advertising

We structure Google Ads campaigns around search intent, keyword quality, campaign priorities, and ad messaging that reflects what the audience is actively looking for rather than broad traffic for its own sake.

Landing page alignment

We review and improve the relationship between the campaign and the page experience so visitors from Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, or remarketing traffic land on a page that feels consistent, credible, and easy to act on.

Testing and optimisation

We run structured testing across audiences, creative angles, copy, campaign setup, and page assumptions so optimisation is based on real signal rather than guesswork or random tweaks.

Tracking and reporting

We review measurement and reporting using known systems such as Google Analytics 4, Google Tag Manager, Meta Pixel, Search Console, and platform-native reporting so decision-makers get clearer visibility without being overwhelmed by unnecessary technical detail.

Commercial insight loop

We translate campaign activity into clear commercial recommendations so you understand what to scale, what to pause, what to improve, and how paid acquisition should connect with the wider digital strategy.

Approach

We move through five practical stages so strategy, execution, and reporting stay tied to the same commercial goals.

01

Account and signal review

Review channels, spend allocation, existing campaigns, tracking quality, page alignment, and the biggest sources of current inefficiency.

02

Channel plan

Define platform mix, campaign priorities, testing logic, budget direction, and what the first performance cycle should validate.

03

Campaign build and activation

Launch the agreed structure across paid social, search, or both, with creative logic and page alignment already mapped to the offer.

04

Test and optimise

Read the signals, refine creative and audiences, improve keyword quality, and tighten the spend against what is performing best.

05

Report and prioritise

Turn campaign data into clear business recommendations so the next decisions on budget, offer, and page experience are easier to make.

Deliverables

What clients actually receive.

  • Channel strategy and campaign priority plan
  • Google Ads and paid social structure recommendations
  • Creative, copy, and audience testing roadmap
  • Landing page and conversion-path improvement notes
  • Reporting format with clear optimisation priorities
  • Recommendations on budget allocation, scaling, and next actions

Best fit

Who this is for.

  • Businesses already investing in acquisition and wanting cleaner scale
  • Teams with underperforming Google Ads, Meta Ads, or mixed-channel campaigns
  • Brands launching new offers that need stronger paid demand capture
  • Companies that need better alignment between ads, pages, and lead quality
  • Service businesses that want more useful reporting for management decisions

Lower waste

Cleaner account structure and tighter optimisation reduce spend going to low-value traffic, weak search intent, or audiences that are unlikely to convert.

Stronger lead quality

Better alignment between ads, offers, and landing pages helps more of the right people reach the next step and improves the quality of inbound demand.

Clearer budget decisions

Better reporting and testing logic make it easier to know which channels deserve more spend, where pages need attention, and what should change before the next cycle.
