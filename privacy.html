Privacy Policy

How ROUNDROCK COTTON s.r.o. collects, uses, and protects information.

Last updated: April 22, 2026. This policy explains how ROUNDROCK COTTON s.r.o. handles personal information collected through this website, contact form submissions, client communications, and related business interactions.

Summary

  • We collect information you voluntarily submit and limited usage data.
  • We use that information to respond to inquiries, deliver services, and improve site performance.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion by contacting us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, company name, phone number, service interests, and any project details submitted through forms, email, or other contact methods. We may also collect limited technical information such as browser type, device information, IP address, and on-site behavior through analytics tools.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to respond to inquiries, evaluate business fit, provide and improve services, communicate about projects, maintain business records, protect site security, and analyze website usage. We may also use information to comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms where necessary.

3. Cookies and Analytics

This website may use cookies or similar technologies to understand traffic patterns, site interactions, and performance trends. These tools help us improve content structure, usability, and the relevance of our marketing. You can adjust your browser settings to manage cookies, though some site features may not function as intended if cookies are disabled.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers that support hosting, analytics, communication, scheduling, or business operations, but only to the extent reasonably necessary for those providers to perform their services. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, maintain business records, resolve disputes, and comply with legal or operational requirements. Retention periods may vary depending on the nature of the relationship and the information involved.

6. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of certain personal information. You may also have the right to object to certain processing activities. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.
