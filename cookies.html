01. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, to provide a better user experience, and to give website operators information about how their site is used.

Cookies can be session cookies, which are temporary and deleted when you close your browser, or persistent cookies, which remain on your device for a set period of time or until you delete them manually. Cookies can be set either by the website you are visiting (first-party cookies) or by third-party services integrated into that website (third-party cookies).

In addition to cookies, we may also use similar technologies such as web beacons, pixels, local storage objects, and scripts for similar purposes. References to "cookies" in this policy encompass all such similar tracking technologies.

This Cookie Policy forms part of our Privacy Policy and should be read in conjunction with it. We are required under the EU ePrivacy Directive and the GDPR to obtain your informed consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device. You can manage your preferences at any time through our cookie consent panel.

02. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to ensure the basic functionality of our website, including navigation, form submission, and security measures; to remember your preferences and settings across visits, improving your experience; to analyse how visitors use our website so that we can improve its structure, content, and performance; to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns and understand which channels drive traffic to our site; to support remarketing and retargeting activities, enabling us to show relevant advertisements to previous website visitors on third-party platforms such as Google and Meta; and to comply with applicable advertising platform policies, including conversion tracking requirements.

03. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as setting your privacy preferences or filling in forms. You cannot opt out of these cookies as the site cannot function without them.

Analytics Cookies: These cookies allow us to count visits and understand how visitors interact with our website. The information collected is aggregated and anonymous. We use this data to improve the website experience. You may opt out of analytics cookies without affecting your ability to use the site.

Marketing Cookies: These cookies are set by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and to show relevant advertisements on other platforms. They work by uniquely identifying your browser. You may opt out of marketing cookies at any time.

Some cookies on our website are placed by third-party services. We do not control these cookies and they are governed by the privacy policies of the respective third parties.

06. Managing Your Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences through the cookie consent panel available in the footer of our website, through your browser settings, through the Google Analytics opt-out tool at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout, through Google Ads settings at https://adssettings.google.com, and through Meta ad preferences at https://www.facebook.com/adpreferences.

Withdrawing consent for non-essential cookies does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing. Strictly necessary cookies cannot be disabled.
